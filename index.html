Ravenclaw is one of the 4 Houses of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. It was founded by Rowena Ravenclaw. Rowena was a scottish witch and one of the four founders of Hogwarts schools. She was noted for her intelligence and creativity as well. The head of the House is Filius Flitwick. Filius Flitwick is a part-goblin wizard who had attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Some time after his graduation he returned to Hogwarts and became the Charms Master as well as head of Ravenclaw House. The members of the House are characterized by their wit, learning and wisdom. Their symbol corresponds to the element of air, this is because the blue and bronze represented the sky and eagle feathers, both of these colors represent the element of air.

Luna Lovegood

Luna Lovegood is a student at Ravenclaw and she was noted for her intelligence.She was raised by her father and had even participated in many battles with the house.

Rowena Ravenclaw

Rowena is the original founder of Ravenclaw and was noted for her intelligence and creativity. Many people say that she was very good friends with all houses. Ravenclaw died sometime when she had became faintly ill at around the 11th century. It is said that she had died a broken heart since her daughter had ran away with her diedem.

Myrtle Warren

Myrtle Warren is a student who was at both Hogwarts and Ravenclaw. She is a ghost who haunts many of the students who bullied her at Hogwarts.
