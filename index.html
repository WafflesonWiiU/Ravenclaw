Ravenclaw is one of the 4 Houses of Hogwarts

=======

Ravenclaw is one of the 4 Houses of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. It was founded by Rowena Ravenclaw. The members of the House are characterized by their wit, learning and wisdom. Their symbol corresponds to the element of air.
